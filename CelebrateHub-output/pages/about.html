About CelebrateHub

We connect people with the finest event services in their area, making every celebration truly extraordinary — from intimate birthdays to grand weddings.

CelebrateHub was founded with a simple mission: to make extraordinary celebrations accessible to everyone. Whether you're planning an intimate birthday dinner or a grand wedding, our platform connects you with vetted, professional vendors in your local area — all in one place.

🎯

Our Mission

To simplify event planning by bringing all celebration services under one roof, making your special moments stress-free and unforgettable for everyone.

🌟

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted event services marketplace, empowering both celebrants and vendors to grow and thrive together through technology.

❤️

Our Values

Trust, quality, and community. Every vendor on our platform is verified, and every service is reviewed by real customers just like you.
