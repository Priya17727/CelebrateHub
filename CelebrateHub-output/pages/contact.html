Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I register as a vendor?

Register an account, then select "Vendor" on the role selection screen. You can immediately start listing your services.

Is CelebrateHub free to use?

Basic listing and browsing is free. We charge a small commission on confirmed bookings to keep the platform running.

How does payment work?

You pay securely through our platform after the vendor accepts your booking request. Multiple payment methods are supported.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes — you can cancel from your "My Bookings" page before the event. Cancellation policies vary by vendor.
